## 🎯 Problem Statement

Are the students AI ready? Do they actually know 
how to use AI in their own specialization?

38 million college graduates enter India's workforce 
every year — but not one carries a verified proof of 
AI readiness. There is no standardized, fair, or 
transparent way for a student to prove their AI 
proficiency. Existing platforms test coding. They test 
theory. But none of them test whether a Commerce student 
from Tripura can actually USE AI in their domain the way 
a company needs them to.

**That is exactly what eraAI solves.**

---

## 🚀 What We Built

A complete AI Proficiency Ecosystem that:
- Tests every student in **their own specialization**
- Covers **6 domains and 24 specializations**
- Uses **4 brutally honest progressive rounds**
- Combines **AI evaluation with human judgment**
- Gives students and recruiters **verified proof**
